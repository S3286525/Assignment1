For my personality profile, I undertook a Myers-Briggs type test, learning style test and creativity test.
See below for results of each individual test and click the links for more detailed info of the test results!.

The results of the tests sum up my personality and habits well.
I am generally an introverted individual, quite a shy person but I do enjoy learning new things and being inquisitive on new concepts or ideas.
I do prefer learning with hands-on activities over theory and I rate my creativity on the average to the lower end of the scale.
The results can influence my behaviour in a team because you need to be more extroverted in a team environment.
In saying that though, I can come across at times as arrogant or a ‘know-it-all’ when expressing myself.
When forming a team, I should focus on inputting my discussions and not be too quiet whilst being respectful of others’ opinions.
Sometimes I do need to think ‘out-of-the-box’ more but at the same time, not to over-analyse everything.
Teamwork is key to success and multiple brains are always better than one.
